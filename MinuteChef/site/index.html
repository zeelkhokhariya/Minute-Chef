MinuteChef Delivery Policy

Our delivery policy is more of a promise.
We are a hard working business group with no money but we will do our best with what we have and get your food to you and not be even a minute late!

Delivery costs:

All item prices are non negotiable and delivery costs are applied to the cart after the order is complete. Delivery price varies with the restaurant's distance from the address given by the customer. If you request for a refund after your food has already been delivered, you will not get the delivery fee back but depending on the reason for the refund request, you would get money back for the order.

Delivery times:

Our policy states that your food will be delivered within the estimated time all the time unless an emergency situation occurs. We revise our delivery times every season to ensure that our estimations are accurate no matter how the weather is. The one situation that we cannot guarantee is snow storms and rain storms as traffic operates differently during those conditions. We have drivers available 24 hours a day and 7 days a week so you only have to worry about your restaurant being open!

Delivery packaging:

How our delivery drivers receive your food is how they deliver it. We are not responsible of the accuracy of the order as we do not open to check ourselves. That is the responsibility of the restaurant but we can help you handle any issues that arise concerning wrong orders or badly done orders.
